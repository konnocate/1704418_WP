Website Storyboard

Layout Design

The website follows a clean, professional layout with consistent navigation and structure across all pages.

Website Pages and Their Purpose

1. Index/Splash Page

Intent: Serves as the main entry point to the portfolio. Provides an immediate introduction through a professional photo and brief overview of my background in technology engineering.

2. Professional Page

Intent: Demonstrates my professional qualifications through my CV, vision statement, mission statement, and detailed career requirements for network security engineering.

3. Personality Page

Intent: Showcases my personal characteristics through personality test results, personal hero analysis, and the impact of my traits on academic life.

4. Personal Development Page

Intent: Documents my growth through reflections on "Twelve Pillars" and highlights my academic achievements in Computer Networking and Cyber Security.

5. Storyboard Page

Intent: Provides technical documentation of the website's design and planning process.

6. References Page

Intent: Lists all sources and citations used in creating the website content in APA format.
